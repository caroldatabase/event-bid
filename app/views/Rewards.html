For Freelancers

Work Work Work

For our service providers we award a bronze, silver and gold service status. The more jobs you complete, the closer you get to each status. Your profile will be marked with a medallion so that the hirers will know that you are a high level service provider.

Reduce your commission & Increase Profits

For every progression in status, you will need to pay a smaller commission rate to Event Bid. We like to reward you fo being an excellent service provider by putting more money in your pocket.
