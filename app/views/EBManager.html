How it works

EB Manager is for those who are wishing to have their entire event organised by one of the EB managers. The EB Manager is there to be your personal assistant in all things surrounding your event. You can engage the EB manager to whatever capacity you wish. They can organise the entire event for you, so all you have to do is show up to you event and enjoy it. They are here to take the stress away from your big day.

Simply fill out a function form and click submit. Once we have received your application, our EB Managers will review what you need and swiftly contact you within 48 hours.

Alternatively, fill out a contact form to arrange a time to chat to one of our EB manager who can help you with everything you need for your event.
